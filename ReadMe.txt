It is  Calculator for expressions
At first go Initializers/database.go and in line dsn write your user,password and database



To calculate expression you need to follow to   "http://localhost:8080/expression/" and enter your data, and you get your ID.
Follow to "http://localhost:8080/calculate" and write your id (your expression begins to count)
Then follow to "http://localhost:8080/result" and write your id, and you will get your result.
